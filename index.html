WHY CHOOSE US

Exciting adventures, beautiful nature, supporting local communities.

Travel and spend a great times with local people, live unique and authentic experiences, learn customs and traditions, and create unforgettable memories!

Unique Experiences

With us, you will live the best experiences ever. Watch and learn about nature, the customs and traditions of the Bedouins and their way of life, and learn more about them through Bedouin experiences such as Arabic coffee and Bedouin bread in ash.

local communities benefit

All employees of the Jordan Trail Guide team come from families living on the Jordan Trail, that’s means 100% of the amount paid by the visitor stays for the local community.

Lowest price guarantee

Our prices are the best in Jordan, because we work by ourselves. We do not organize trips and sell them and take a commission like other companies. No, we work by ourselves.

Start gazing

After dinner in the middle of the desert , you can experience the magical night sky in all its glory. With no other sources of light, the sky is lit only by thousands of twinkling stars and planets with occasional shooting stars burning their tails into the night sky . Our guides will navigate you through the solar system, sharing stories of the constellations and exploring how Bedouins use the stars navigate through the desert
