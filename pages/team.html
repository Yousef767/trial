OUR TEAM

Together We Climb,
Together We Thrive

Everyone in the Jordan Trail Guide Agency team love exploring and are very passionate about what they do.  We are here for you to create a real local experience and memories that last forever. With us, you can not only have a great hiking adventure on Jordan trail, but you can visit and be part of local families' everyday life, learn about their customs and traditions, sleep under the millions of stars deep in the desert, cook local Arabic food and really experience the true nature of Jordan. We aim to offer you full-service solutions, and give our best to create for you and your friends the most authentic trip like never before. Let us take care of all the arrangements, so you can really enjoy every moment and don't need to worry about any details.
