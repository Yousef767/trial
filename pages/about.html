WHO WE ARE

We Are the Bridge to Your Ridge Trek Dreams.

The Jordan Trail Guide is a local organisation, completely managed by a team from the local community who work on their own to support their families and children. The owner of the Jordan Trail Guide organisation is Essa Alammarin, a local guide . He was one of the first 3 people to walk the Jordan Trail from Umm Qais to Aqaba after it was established in 2015, and still leads visitors himself on the trail. Essa and his team create unique, authentic, and unforgettable experiences for visitors to the Jordan Trail - a journey through Jordan’s diverse nature, culture and traditions." The Jordan Trail Guide organisation cooperates with local partners from Amman, Aqaba and Wadi Rum to create full service experience".
